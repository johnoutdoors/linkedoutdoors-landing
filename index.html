What makes us different

Built for people who actually go outside

Everything you loved about outdoor Facebook groups, rebuilt from scratch without the garbage that drove you away.

📍
Hyperlocal Feed

Your feed shows content from your region and your interests — nothing else. A fishing report from the Chesapeake Bay won't compete with noise from across the country.

🎣
Activity Filtering

Fishing, hiking, kayaking, hunting, mountain biking — filter by what you do. Drill into sub-categories like bay fishing vs. stream fishing vs. fly fishing.

⏱️
Chronological, Always

No algorithm decides what you see. Posts appear in the order they were posted. If someone updated trail conditions an hour ago, you see it at the top.

👍
Helpful — Not Likes

Posts get "Helpful" votes, not likes. A detailed fishing report with conditions and gear gets upvoted. A vague selfie doesn't. The community rewards quality.

📅
Local Events & Meetups

Saturday morning hikes. Dawn patrol fishing meetups. Trail cleanups. Find and RSVP to events in your area, or create your own with capacity limits and waitlists.

⭐
Reputation That Means Something

Earn ranks from Trail Hand to Master Guide based on the quality of your contributions. Expert badges tell newcomers exactly who to trust for local knowledge.
